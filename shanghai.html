My Story in Shanghai

Shanghai is the most modern city in China, and it was built to look like New York City. Shanghai is the most desired city by most young Chinese, not only for its economy but also for its modern architecture. Although I think living in Shanghai can be a bit exhausting, I have to admit it's a very nice city to visit!

Attractions

Shanghai Disneyland

Shanghai Disneyland is a theme park located in Chuansha New Town which is part of the Shanghai Disney Resort. It was opened in June 2016 and it is the first Disneyland Park in China mainland until now.

Shanghai CBD

Shanghai CBD is one of the most famous business district in the world. It is located next to the Huangpu river which is the heart area of Shanghai city. There are three skyscrapers in Shanghai CBD which are Shanghai Trade Tower, Shanghai Oriental Pearl TV Tower and Jinnah tower.

Food

Xiaolongbao

Shanghai Xiaolongbao refers to a type of small steamed bun traditionally prepared in a xiaolong, a small bamboo steaming basket. Shanghai xiaolongbao is the most famous steamed bun in China.
