My Story in Sanya

Sanya is the southernmost city in China, it faces the sea and is the largest tourist city in China. It has some of the best beaches and coastline in China, much like Miami, and before I came to study at the University of Miami, my parents would bring me to Hainan for vacation every summer period. (When I came to college, I never went back. After all, I lived every day in the "Hainan" part of the United States.) But Hainan is still a great place to go, there is a lot of seafood, and it's a great place to vacation!

Attractions

Yalong Bay

Yalong Bay is a 7.5 kilometres beach located in southeast Sanya. It is also known as the Yalong Bay National Resort. There are serveral famous luxury hotels located at Yalong Bay includes Hilton, Marriott, Ritz Carlton and Edition Hotel as know as ‘Chinese south beach’.

Food

Coconut Chicken

Hainan Coconut Chicken soup is a traditional southern Chinese food. Boiling fresh coconut water with other ingredients in the pot and put the chicken into the soup.
